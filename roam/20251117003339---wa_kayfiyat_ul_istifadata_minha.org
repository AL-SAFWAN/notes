#+title: wa kayfiyat ul-istifadata minha

wa kayfiyat ul-istifadata minha "and how to make full use of them" 


refers to
- the understanding and application of methodologies
  - that enables a jurist ([[id:0e48c744-3e6e-4d6a-9b43-5a43daf938e1][faqih]])
    - to extract (interpret) and apply (implement) rulings from the legal evidences efficiently.

:PROPERTIES:

:END:
** discerning the general from the specific
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       49848bac-889f-4ea8-b21f-67bec4d108f0
:END:
