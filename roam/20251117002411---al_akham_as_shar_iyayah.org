#+title: al-ahkam as shar'iyayah
#+filetags: :terminology:

[[id:948d05ba-0ec0-403a-b737-6f79eab474d2][al-akham]] as shar'iyayah
refers to legal rulings derived from religious legislation. These include determinations of what is obligatory or prohibited.

It specifically excludes:
intellectual judgement, such as understanding that a whole is greater than any of it's part, and natural observation like the occurrence of rain on a clear winter night.
