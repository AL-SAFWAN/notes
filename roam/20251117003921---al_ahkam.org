#+title: Al-ahkam

The term "[[id:948d05ba-0ec0-403a-b737-6f79eab474d2][Al-ahkam]]" (rulings), in its plural form derived form "[[id:a052cfd1-2d00-476f-9df6-036c999d7f1b][hukm]]", refers linguistically to a judgement or decision.

In the context of Islamic jurisprudence, it specifically denotes judgements that arise from the directives issued by the legislator - the Lawgiver, which in this instance are manifested in the Quran and the Sunnah.

These directive address the actions of those who are deemed legally responsible (al-[[id:ba93d19b-37c1-40d2-9613-a01ac674fa21][mukallaf]]in) with the Islamic law.

Such rulings mandate specific behaviour and choices or outline certain state, providing a structured guide on how to conduct oneself in accordance with divine legislation.
