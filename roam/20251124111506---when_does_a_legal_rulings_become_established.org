#+title: When does a legal rulings become established?

Absence of Mawani’ (preventers)
Presence of Shurut (resons)
Existence of Asbab (varriers)
-----
A legal ruling is established when three conditions are
fulfilled:
1. Existence of Asbab (Causes): The necessary causes
must be present.
2. Presence of Shurut (Conditions): The required
conditions must be met.
3. Absence of Mawani’ (Barriers): Any barriers must be
absent
