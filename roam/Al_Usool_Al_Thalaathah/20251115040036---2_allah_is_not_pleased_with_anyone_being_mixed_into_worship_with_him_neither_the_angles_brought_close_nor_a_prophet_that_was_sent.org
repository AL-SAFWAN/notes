#+title: 2. Allah is not pleased with anyone being mixed into worship with him - neither the angles brought close nor a prophet that was sent

The proof for this is:
 #+begin_quote
 [[id:d3a58b0a-7ac7-4d5f-a4dc-1c4b801756e8][The Jinn (72:18)]] 
 And [He revealed] that the masjids are for Allāh, so do not invoke (or worship) with Allāh anyone.
 — Saheeh International
 Footnote - 1
 The term "masjid" here includes every place of worship or the earth in general.
 https://quran.com/72/18
 #+end_quote
