The Jinn (72:18) 

وَأَنَّ ٱلْمَسَـٰجِدَ لِلَّهِ فَلَا تَدْعُوا۟ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ أَحَدًۭا ١٨

And [He revealed] that the masjids are for Allāh, so do not invoke with Allāh anyone.
— Saheeh International

Footnote - 1
 The term "masjid" here includes every place of worship or the earth in general.
