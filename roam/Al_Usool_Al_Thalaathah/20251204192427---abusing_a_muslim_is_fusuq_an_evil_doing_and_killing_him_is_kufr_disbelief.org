#+title: Abusing a Muslim is Fusuq (an evil doing) and killing him is Kufr (disbelief).
#+filetags: :hadith:

حَدَّثَنَا مُحَمَّدُ بْنُ عَرْعَرَةَ، قَالَ حَدَّثَنَا شُعْبَةُ، عَنْ زُبَيْدٍ، قَالَ سَأَلْتُ أَبَا وَائِلٍ عَنِ الْمُرْجِئَةِ،، فَقَالَ حَدَّثَنِي عَبْدُ اللَّهِ، أَنَّ النَّبِيَّ صلى الله عليه وسلم قَالَ ‏ "‏ سِبَابُ الْمُسْلِمِ فُسُوقٌ، وَقِتَالُهُ كُفْرٌ ‏"‏‏.‏

Narrated 'Abdullah: 
The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Abusing a Muslim is Fusuq (an evil doing) and killing him is Kufr (disbelief)."

Sahih al-Bukhari 48
Chapter 36: (What is said regarding) the fear of a believer that his good deeds may be annulled (lost) without his knowledge, Book 2: Belief
