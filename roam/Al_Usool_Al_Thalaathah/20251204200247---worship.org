#+title: worship 
* What is worship

** Definition

*** Linguistic definition
- to lower and humble oneself to the greater Being with humility, to submit to a greater Being.

*** Technical definition
- a comprehensive word that encompasses everything that Allah loves and that which He is pleased with
  - from the statements and actions
    - the apparent of them and the hidden of them

**** Apparent and hidden acts of worship

- the apparent is the statement of the tongue and the outward actions
  - prayer, doing Hajj
- the hidden is what the heart testifies to
  - belief in Allah, the Last Day, the Prophet (ï·º)
