7. The types of Taqlīd :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

/A general knowledge of/:
- the *evidences* of fiqh,
- the *manner* of deriving rulings from them,
- and the *standing* of the individual who is able to derive.

/Three pillars/
1. *Evidences of Fiqh* (األدلة)
2. *The manner of deriving rulings* – /dalālāt al-alfāẓ/ (دالالت األلفاظ)
3. *The one able to derive rulings* – /al-mujtahid/ (المجتهد)

    
* Taqlīd (تقليد) – following qualified opinion
