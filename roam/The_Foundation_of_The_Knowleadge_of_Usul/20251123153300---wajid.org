- Haram - Forbidden
  - Actions that the legislator has explicitly prohibited.
- Makruh - Disliked
  - Actions that are discouraged but not sinful if performed
- Mubah - Permissible
  - (Actions that are neither required nor discouraged, and carry no reward of punishment)
- Mandud - That are Encouraged
- Sunnah - acts that are recommended but not obligatory.
  - Performing these actions brings reward, whereas neglecting them does not bring sin. This clarification is essential to differentiate between various levels of legal and moral responsibilities in Islamic law
