#+title: Forms of command
The forms of command are four:
* 1. The [[id:a89960c3-6006-48e7-bb57-570385e12756][imperative verb]], such as
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       d3629be3-5a3b-4b2b-8e3c-c1e2e4670cde
:END:
   "recite [O Muhammed], what has been revealed to you of the book" 29:45
   - recite, sit
* 2. The [[id:a89960c3-6006-48e7-bb57-570385e12756][imperative verbal]] [[id:555a5917-a056-4a45-b474-826991512081][noun]]
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       83bbc6e0-bbcd-4fb5-a295-50ae73548c5a
:END:
, such as
   "Come to prayer" (from the adhan)
   - stop, come
* 3. The verbal [[id:555a5917-a056-4a45-b474-826991512081][noun]] that is used in place of the [[id:a89960c3-6006-48e7-bb57-570385e12756][imperative verb]]
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       f92998c1-f1aa-4e94-aa5f-5874e543a82c
:END:
, such as:
   "So when you meet those who disbelieve [in battle], strike [their] necks' 47:4
   - strike the neck, have patients 
* 4. [[id:2c68166d-d56e-4c7a-b320-515a3e7df4a8][Present tense verb]] joined with the [[id:3168ace7-d627-4c16-8c61-d354691251cd][Lam]] of imperative (لام الأمر).
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       41452bd9-ce6f-42fe-9528-e0a4c7c7cd3f
:END:
,such as:
   "To believe in Allah and his messenger" 58:4
   - believe in allah and the messenger

* Commands can also be conveyed through means other than the standard forms mentioned above,
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       60367b02-241e-4049-aaf0-e606ec57a63a
:END:
- for instance,a command may be indicated by
** describing it
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       aae597b7-b1f9-4219-acc5-2759ebea483a
:END:
   - as fard, wajib, mandub or obedience,
     - or by praising the performer and
       - disparaging the one who neglects it.
** reward/punishment associated
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       41f16c99-d3ab-4026-96da-052b8008f90f
:END:
  - with its fulfilment,
    - ore leaving it may incur punishment.
