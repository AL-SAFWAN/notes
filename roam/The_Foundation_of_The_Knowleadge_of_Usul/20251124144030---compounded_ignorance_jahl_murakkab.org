#+title: Compounded ignorance (Jahl murakkab)

- Compounded Ignorance
  - Opposing Clear Evidence
  - Incorrect Deduction
    - reason why people come about with bidah
      - he believes it's correct e.g. christains
        
  - Presumption of Correctness
  - Cannot Validate rulings

Definition:
“It is the perception of something in a way that contradicts how it truly is.”

“Jahl Murakkab”, refers to a state of misunderstanding or misperception where an individual not only lacks knowledge about a subject but also holds a false belief about it.
