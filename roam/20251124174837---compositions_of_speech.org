Speech is control from the basic element of language, which include their component/elements and their parts/meaning 

* Definition
- *Linguistically*: “Words composed to articulate meaning.”
- *Technically*: “Words that are meaningful.”

* Classifications of speech
** By its components ([[id:820d3e4a-d797-4d4f-a4a6-cb1b74dba462][Element of speech)]]
- Nouns
- Verbs
- Particles
** By its meaning ([[id:5496fa89-1a4b-4d3b-8b72-224954f7ee33][Parts of speech]])
- Speech that can be described as truthful or false.
- Speech that cannot be described as either truthful or untruthful.

** By its usage  ([[id:6bd8484a-2995-4d55-bc50-addb39677c23][Literal words and Metaphors)]]
- Literal (ḥaqīqah)
- Metaphors (majāz)
