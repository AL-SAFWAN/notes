Definition:

“It is the perception of something as it truly is, with firm conviction.”

- For instance, understanding that the whole is greater than the part, or recognising that intention is a prerequisite for the acceptance of worship.


“al-Ilm” implies understanding or comprehending something with certainty, as it actually exists.
