#+title: Al insha

speech that are not concerned with truthfulness but rather with expressing commands, requests, prohibitions, wishes, or questions.
- non-informative, but performative.
  - it seeks to produce an action, response, or particular sentiment.
  
- Examples of insha include
  - phrases like “Stand up!” (a command) or “Do not sit!” (a prohibition),
  - As in the saying of the Most High: "Worship Allah and associate nothing with him" 4:36
