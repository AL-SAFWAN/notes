* Preventions / Obstacles to Legal Accountability (موانع التكليف)

** Main obstacles
- الجهل :: *Ignorance* – not knowing the ruling or not having learnt it.
- النسيان :: *Forgetfulness* – genuinely forgetting the ruling at the time of action.
- الإكراه :: *Coercion* – being forced to do something under compulsion.


* KILL Summary of the Mukallaf – The one held accountable
- The *mukallaf* is the one who is held accountable for all *commands* and *prohibitions* of the Sharī‘ah.
- A person becomes a *mukallaf* when *any one* of the following signs of maturity appears:
  1. Reaching 15 lunar years.
  2. Growth of hair around the front private organ.
  3. Releasing seminal fluid.
  4. Menstruation.
  5. Pregnancy.
- The main preventions of accountability are:
  1. Ignorance.
  2. Forgetfulness.
  3. Coercion.
