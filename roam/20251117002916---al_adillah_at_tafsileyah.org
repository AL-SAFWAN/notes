#+title: al-adillah at tafsileyah
#+filetags: :terminology:
The term "specific evidences" (al-adillah at tafsileyah) refers to evidence in fiqh that are specifically related to detailed aspects of fiqh.

This definition excludes the subject of usul ul fiqh which deals primaryly with the general principles and broader evidence within fiqh.
