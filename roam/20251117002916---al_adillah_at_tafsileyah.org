#+title: al-adillah at tafsileyah
#+filetags: :terminology:

al-adillah at-tafsileyah "specific evidences"

refers to
- evidences specifically related to detailed aspects of fiqh.

excludes:
- the subject of usul ul fiqh
  - which deals primarily with the general principles 
  - broader evidence within fiqh.
