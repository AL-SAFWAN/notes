#+title: Underwheling Perception Wahm

- Underwhelming Perception
  - Possible Ambiguity in Evidence
  - Correct/Incorrect Deduction
  - Greater Uncertainty
  - Cannot Validate rulings


Definition:
“It is the perception of something with the possibility of a stronger opposing view.”
1%-49%
“al-Wahm” indicates a state of understanding or awareness of a particular thing while also recognising the likelihood or probability of an opposing perspective that is considered stronger or more convincing.
