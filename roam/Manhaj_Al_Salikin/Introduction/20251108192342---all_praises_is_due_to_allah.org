#+title: All praises is due to Allah

Praising Allah, because Allah is deserving of praise, and it is not like sukur where you thank Allah for giving you something, but for his greatness

Al-hamd -> All types of hamd/ magnifying and glorying Allah (ﷻ) with thanks and love from the beginning of time and till the end of time from every location encapsulates this.

lilah -> Ownership, it belongs to Allah (ﷻ)
