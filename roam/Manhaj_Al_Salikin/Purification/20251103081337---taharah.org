#+title: taharah
#+filetags: :terminology:

taharah mean purification

to lift the state of [[id:24cec8cd-6ee0-46e1-b7a5-337a4a72b8aa][hadath]] or remove [[id:eaaaa701-b527-4002-8e43-c7b9e809478f][najis]]
e.g. making wudo - lifting the state of hadath
e.g. removing najis - lifting the state of najasa
