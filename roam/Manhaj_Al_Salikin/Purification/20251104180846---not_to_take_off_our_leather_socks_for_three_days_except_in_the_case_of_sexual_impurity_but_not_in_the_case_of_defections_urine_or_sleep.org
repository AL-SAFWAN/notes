#+title: not to take off our leather socks for three days except in the case of sexual impurity, but not in the case of defections, urine or sleep 
The Messenger of Allah peace and blessing of Allah be on him would not command us not to take off our leather socks for three days except in the case of sexual impurity, but not in the case of defections, urine or sleep (i.e. during traveling) (Thirmidhi and Nasaâ€™i)
