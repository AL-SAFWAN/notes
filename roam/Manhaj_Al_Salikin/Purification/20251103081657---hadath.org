#+title: hadath
#+filetags: :terminology:

The state of ritual impurities (that a person enters), that prevents him from Salah.

a state that affects you spiritually, even if your body is physically clean. Can be minor (wudo/tayyamum) or major (ghusl/tayyamum)
- you can be free of najasah but still in hadath
- or have not hadith, but still have najasah on your clothes
  - when you make wudo, you lift yourself from hadith, but not the najasah that will require removing the najis
