#+title: The default position is purity
- [[id:b09bcecf-f000-473a-bdfa-42f867cb51cc][All things are pure by default, unless proven otherwise]]
- What seems to be correct is that blood is pure, due to the many incidence of the life time of the sahabah and did not see it as impure, in terms of action and evidence. 
  - During jihad, they had stains of blood
  - likewise the one who is martyr 
  - The Prophet (ﷺ) did not prohibit bleeding companions from entering the masjid.
    - Example: When a companion was struck by an arrow and his vein was bleeding, the Prophet (ﷺ) set up a tent inside the masjid. 
  - Abu Hurayrah experienced a nosebleed.
  - Ibn ʿUmar popped a pimple/spot and it bled — and he did not wash it as najasah.
  - Scholars: Hasan al basri
