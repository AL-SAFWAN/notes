#+title: 5 types of rulings (ahkam pl. of hukm)
#+roam_alias: "ahkam" "hukm"
#+filetags: :terminology:fiqh:

* Wajib (compulsory)
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       3a023159-3a58-4e9b-9bf1-a5cadc9b8660
:END:
- Same as Fard and Laazim
That by which is doer is rewarded and one who leaves it is punished

* Haram (prohibited)
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       aaabccc4-2540-4838-a7b3-3ff874614ad4
:END:
- same as Muharam
It is the opposite of the Wajib
If you did it you deserve to be punished, and if you leave it you are rewarded.

* Makruh (disliked)
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       6e1dcc2a-8e45-4d48-87e0-ba3878674725
:END:
Actions that one is rewarded for leaving but not punished for doing

* Masnun (recommended)
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       4ae002c4-75f5-4a2a-a8c6-2e39fb67dbae
:END:
- Same Sunanh, Mustahab and Nafl
The opposite to Makruh
Actions that is reward for doing it, but not sinful for leaving it

* Mubah (permissible):
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       e68929d3-b4a4-46f4-b2e6-61a97a2d5d62
:END:
An actions where its doing or leaving is considered the same
