#+title: Element of speech


In the Arabic language,
- a single unit of speech is a word
speech is structured around three primary components:
   - [[id:555a5917-a056-4a45-b474-826991512081][nouns]] - ism - ‫األسماء‬
     (The structure of speech in linguistic terms continues with verb and particle, which further enrich how meaning are conveyed)
   - [[id:2c5eb58e-075f-44e4-b0c9-7e52d26f92aa][verbs]] - fi‘l - ‫األفعال‬
   - [[id:fa9b199b-9494-4857-b7da-ab7d599b6382][particles]] - ḥarf - ‫الحروف‬

typically involving at least two components; two nouns or a combination of a verb and noun.

The Categories of Speech in its *Minimal Composition* to give a meaning that we can benefit from:

- Noun & Particle
        O Zayd – ‫زيد‬ ‫يا‬
        O Fatimah – ‫فاطمة‬ ‫يا‬
- Verb & Particle
        He did not come –
        She did not eat –
- Verb & Noun
        Zayd wrote –
        Fatimah slept –
- Two Nouns
        Zayd (is a) Teacher – ‫مدرس‬ ‫زيد‬,
        Fatimah (is a) Doctor – ‫طبيبة‬ ‫فاطمة‬
