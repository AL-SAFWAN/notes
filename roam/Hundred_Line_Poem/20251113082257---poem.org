#+title: poem

Each line is referred to as a bayt. [[id:b3f38ad0-6337-4f33-a063-672fb3019c49][Bayt]] means house. plural, Bayut (grammatical), Abiyat (in poem).

In poetry it means the entire line. Each part of the Bayt, is know [[id:a2bd4801-e9c1-402e-97d5-57b8f6c7e759][statrul awwal]](1st part), or [[id:36ad67a0-c47f-4af2-8b1b-72a5c5f5c872][statrul thani(2nd part)]].
