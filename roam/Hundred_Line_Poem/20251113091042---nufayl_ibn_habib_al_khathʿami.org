#+title: Nufayl ibn Habib al-Khathʿami

- tribe: Khathʿam.
- He loved the ka'bah
- tried to mobilise his people to resist, but after seeing the size of the army, he realised they could not fight.
- He was captured and initially sentenced to death, but Abraha spared him and kept him as a guide, forcing him to show the army the route toward Makkah.
